The Opportunity

A Narrow Window
for Maximum Recovery

Federal court rulings and CBP administrative guidance have opened significant refund opportunities for importers who paid duties under IEEPA-based tariff orders. This window is limited — and most importers are unaware it exists.

01

Court-Ordered Refunds

Recent federal rulings have created legal pathways to recover duties paid on eligible import entries across multiple HTS classifications.

02

CBP CAPE Portal

The newly launched CBP CAPE portal is the primary filing channel for eligible refund claims. Proper preparation before filing is critical to maximizing recovery.

03

Time-Limited Eligibility

Claim windows are tied to entry dates and regulatory deadlines. Every week of delay narrows your eligible entry pool and reduces total recovery potential.

Who Qualifies

U.S. importers who paid IEEPA-related duties on eligible entries from China, Vietnam, Taiwan, South Korea, or other affected origins may qualify for significant refunds. Foreign manufacturers operating as Importer of Record (IOR) under DDP terms are also eligible.

Our preliminary assessment process identifies your eligibility, estimates your refund pool, and maps your highest-value entry clusters — before you commit to a full recovery engagement.
